1.0 Interactive Insights: Transforming HDB Resale Pricing Analysis
By Shawn Ng & Jordan Tay

Background
Our project aims to improve accessibility and understanding of HDB resale pricing data by using interactive data visualization.

Current formats provided by government sources like HDB Resale Statistics and popular property portals such as Property Guru, Edgeprop, 99.co, and Stacked, though informative, lack interactivity and visual engagement beyond basic tables and heat maps.

By addressing this gap, we seek to empower users with dynamic exploration capabilities, facilitating deeper insights into market trends and enabling more informed decision-making for buyers, sellers, and relevant stakeholders in the real estate sector.


Research Question:
How can we use Data Visualization to gain insight into HDB Resale prices? We answer this question in 3 parts:

1. Creating HDB Resale Price Chart that helps stakeholders easily identify prices and towns within a specific budget.
Source: HDB Resale Flat Prices Link

2. A genealogy of HDB Resale price including variables & sources:
a) HDB Price Index (Pricing)Link
b) Interest rates (Financing)Link
c) Household income (Financing)Link
d) Population changes (Demand)Link
e) HDB completions (Supply)Link
f) Cooling measures (Interventions)Link
e) HDB Rental [OPTIONAL]Link

3) 99 Year leasehold HDB Blocks
Over the years, there has been increasing concern over lease expiry in Singapore. The government has officially made clear that that will be no lease extension or renewal for flat facing expiry. Hence, lease depreciation has been an increasing hot button topic. We seek to explore and create a geospatial visualization of all HDB blocks in Singapore and their leasehold status


Methodology
Using various secondary data links as listed above, we intend to collate, clean and compare the relevant data in our data visualization.
For geospatial analysis of HDB Blocks with 99 year leasehold, we intend to use an API to connect to Onemap / HDB in order to get coordinate information on each HDB block.
